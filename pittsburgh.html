Welcome to Pittsburgh!

Pittsburgh is best known for its steel production back in the 1900's (which is how it got its nickname of the "Steel City"), and today for its sports teams as well as being a great place to live in. Pittsburgh is located in Allegheny County in southwestern Pennsylvania. This urban city was incorporated in 1816 and has a population of 303,034 as of the year 2024. Compared to the rest of the state's average household income of $80,996, Pittsburgh is above that number with $87,811 as the average.

Pittsburgh is a major center for culture, education, healthcare and technology, not only in the state, but throughout the country! It is home to world-class universities such as the University of Pittsburgh and Carnegie Mellon, and boasts some of the best medical facilities in regards to heart and children's health. Pittsburgh has it all, from historic charm to modern amenities, which keeps its tourism industry booming. There are plenty of incredible dining options available in downtown Pittsburgh, with great views of the three rivers that flow around it (which is also why they call it the "Confluence"). Pittsburgh is home to 3 major sports teams (Hockey: Pittsburgh Penguins, Football: Pittsburgh Steelers, Baseball: Pittsburgh Pirates).
