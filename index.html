Parks In Pennsylvania

Pennsylvania is home to 124 State Parks and 19 National Parks, each with its own rich history and stunning scenery. There are various natural wonders hidden throughout these parks, such as waterfalls, lakes, and unique rock formations. There's always something to explore! With over 800 campgrounds located throughout the state, you can stay close to nature and take in the sights and sounds of everything Pennsylvania has to offer.

    Top 5 Biggest Parks in PA By Acreage:

  1. Pymatuning State Park - 21,122 acres
  2. Bucktail State Park Natural Area - 21,039 acres
  3. Ohiopyle State Park - 19,052 acres
  4. Moraine State Park - 16,725 acres
  5. Hickory Run State Park - 15,550 acres
We're glad you're here!

Welcome to Pennsylvania, otherwise known as the "Commonwealth"! Located between the Mid-Atlantic and Northeastern regions, our state is filled with a rich blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. If you are exploring the western side of the state, you'll find the beautiful Appalachian Mountains a great place to hike. If you venture over to the eastern side of the state, you can explore cities like Philadelphia, which contains many important landmarks of American independence (such as the Liberty Bell) and visit some of America's first establishments (America's first zoo, hospital, and public library). There is always something to explore here! We hope you come to visit!
